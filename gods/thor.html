Thor, Slayer of Giants

Thor is one of the most revered gods in Norse mythology, possibly even rivaling Odin. He is depicted as one of the strongest among gods and giants, and many tales include him slaying one or a great number of giants. To assist him in these battles, he has the hammer Mjolnir at his side, a magical artifact created by the dwarves that will always return to Thor when thrown.

Thor is fated to battle the world serpent, Jormungandr, at Ragnarok. Thor will kill the serpent with his hammer, but will not make it but a couple steps before falling dead to Jormungandr's poison.
