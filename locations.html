The Locations of Norse Mythology

A World Unlike Our Own

Norse Mythology was developed far in the past, when things such as world maps simply didn't exist. Myths and legends are often created to explain the mysteries humans have no way of explaining on their own. One such mystery was this: What does the world look like? What does the universe look like?

To answer this question, the Norse came up with the world tree, Yggdrasil, which held nine realms. At the center of them all was Earth, or as they called it, Midgard.
