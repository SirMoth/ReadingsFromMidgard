Midgard

Oluf Bagge, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons
Pronunciation
Old Norse: Miðgarðr
Modern English: Midgard

Midgard, the Middle Enclosure

Midgard is the physical realm, and the only of the nine realms that humans can reach. Put simply, Midgard is earth. It is described as surrounded by a great ocean, where the world serpent Jormungandr resides. The creation myth describes how Midgard was created from the remains of the first frost giant, Ymir.
